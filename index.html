Patrick Wright | Guitarist

ABOUT ME

Born and raised in Indianapolis, I have been playing guitar for over 20 years. Styles include jazz, fusion, classical, punk, and funk among many others. I have performed with musicians such as Christian McBride and Donny Hodges. I currently reside and perform in the Indianapolis area.

I teach guitar, bass guitar, flute, and ukulele at Carmel Music Center, and am currently accepting new students.

My influences include:

  • John McLaughlin
  • Wes Montgomery
  • Jeff Beck
  • Pat Metheny
  • Alex Lifeson

 I'm looking forward to and seeking new opportunities in the industry. For all inquiries, please contact me.
