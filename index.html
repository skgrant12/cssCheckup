Patrick Wright | Guitarist

About Me

Hi! I'm Patrick. Born and raised in Indianapolis, I have been playing guitar for over 20 years. Styles include jazz, fusion, classical, punk, and funk among many others. I have performed with musicians such as Christian McBride and Fareed Haque. I currently reside in Indianapolis, teaching guitar, bass, flute, ukulele, and rock band at Bach to Rock Westfield and performing around town. In my spare time, I love traveling with my Fiance, Sarah, and hanging out with my cats Crispy and Pumpkin.

For all inquiries, please contact me.
